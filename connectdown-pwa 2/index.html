The Basics

How To Score

It is not complicated. In fact the whole point is that it should not be.

Step 1
Show Up

Go somewhere. A cafe, a park, a bar, literally anywhere. Put your phone in your pocket. Look up.

Step 2
Say Something

It does not need to be clever. It does not need to be perfect. It just needs to be real. A comment. A question. A bad joke. Whatever works.

Step 3
Score a Connectdown

If you end up in a real conversation, even for 5 minutes, that is a Connectdown. Post it to the Scoreboard. You are on the board.
